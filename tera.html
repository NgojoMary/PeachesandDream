The Earth's Round Anyway
I often ask myself,
Am I taking the wrong way,
That’s when I remember the Earth is round
So I shouldn’t turn around
And just keep on walking anyway,
In hopes to find what I’ve always been looking for

I’ll carry on until my feet can’t carry me anymore,
I’ll work on until my muscles sore
I’ll fly high until my wings distort
I’ll fail as much as I want until I can gain more
People try to stop me, telling me I’m crazy,
I don’t bother listening to what they say,
I keep my determination at bay
My perseverance can not be swayed
The only thing left to do is pray,
And hope it is the right path.

I often ask myself,
Am I doing the right thing?
Always questioning what I’m doing.
Is it good, is it bad?
Would it make me happy
or would it make me sad?
I’m losing my mind to find the answer
I’m too lost to wonder
Now, will only look forward and run faster
No matter how many times I drop,
I always told myself to get up
My mind is possessed
By the word success
Because giving up
Was never in my vocabulary
I can taste my own downfalls
And stand alone voluntarily
I never look back
and keep on moving higher to the top
I may fail again
But I always remember
that the Earth is round anyway.
I can crawl my way up to the top again
And this time, find a way to stay.
